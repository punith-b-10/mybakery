Our Story

Made in a Kitchen
Filled with Love

Two Brothers Home Bakery began as a love affair with ingredients — real butter, single-origin chocolate, farm eggs, and no shortcuts. Every dessert is prepared fresh, by hand, only after you place your order. Nothing sits on a shelf. Nothing contains preservatives.

We believe great desserts deserve time. That's why we work in small batches, perfecting each recipe until it's exactly right. Whether it's a single jar of peanut butter or a hundred custom chocolates for a wedding — every order receives the same devotion.

For individual orders, 24 hours notice is ideal. For bulk orders (events, weddings, corporate), we recommend 48–72 hours in advance. For very large orders (100+ pieces), please give us at least a week's notice.

Tiramisu and cheesecakes are best within 2–3 days when refrigerated. Cookies and brownies stay fresh for 3–4 days in an airtight container. Peanut butter lasts up to 2 weeks refrigerated.
Yes — we deliver within Bangalore. Delivery charges vary by distance. For pickups, we can arrange a convenient location. Please mention your area when ordering and we'll confirm logistics.
